Bangladesh is calling

More than 5.8 million people in Bangladesh have been impacted by the worst flood to hit the region in three decades. At least 70 people have lost their lives to the flood in the eleven districts - Feni, Noakhali, Khagrachari, Cumilla, Brahmanbaria, Chattogram, Moulvibazar, Habiganj, Sylhet, Lakshmipur and Cox's Bazar in eastern Bangladesh.

Houses, crops, livestock and fisheries have been washed away, affecting immediate livelihood options as well as leaving lasting impacts. More than 150,000 tube wells and nearly as many latrines have been destroyed - pushing the region alarmingly close to a public health crisis.

We are on the ground — supporting people with emergency relief and rehabilitation. We are urging individuals and organisations to come forward and stand beside us, as we stand with the people who need it the most.
