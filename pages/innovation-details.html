Mobile Health Clinics

Started: January 2023
To address the lack of healthcare in remote areas, our NGO launched the Mobile Health Clinic project, using solar-powered vans equipped with essential medical tools and staff. These clinics travel weekly to reach underserved communities, offering basic check-ups, maternal care, vaccinations, and health education. This project is transforming lives by ensuring that distance is no longer a barrier to healthcare.
